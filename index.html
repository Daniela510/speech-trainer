How to play?

Click "start" to get a prompt. Then for 2 minutes talk about the subject, its that easy!

This is a tool so there are some goals other than the time:

  • Be coherent, your speech must flow.
  • No "uhms", "eeh", "ugh", these disqualify the speech.
  • Incorporate the spice words given underneath.

A successfull game is a nice speech that flows with no uhms & ughs. If someone were to listen to you, they must not understand what were the spice words. You can record yourself or play with friends to have feedback on how are you doing and hold you accountable for all the uhms & ughs.

Not so easy once you try, huh?
